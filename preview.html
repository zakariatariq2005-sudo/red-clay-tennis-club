FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Memberships are annual commitments with monthly payment options. Court booking privileges depend on membership tier. All members must follow club etiquette and court maintenance protocols.
  • Individual members can book up to 4 hours per week in advance. Family memberships allow 8 hours per week. Founding members receive priority booking and extended limits.
  • Coaching availability varies by programme. Private coaching sessions are scheduled based on coach and member availability. Young Adult Programme includes structured weekly sessions.
  • Clay courts require maintenance after rain. Courts are closed during and immediately after precipitation. Members receive notifications about court availability. Indoor training facilities remain available.
  • Non-marking tennis shoes are required on all courts. Clay-specific shoes are recommended but not mandatory. Street shoes are prohibited on court surfaces.
  • Family plans cover up to 4 family members. All members must be registered. Junior programme access is included. Guest privileges are shared across the family membership.
