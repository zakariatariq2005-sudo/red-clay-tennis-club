Why train at RED CLAY
is the choice of champions

Red clay courts are rare in Pakistan. Our ITF-certified surfaces offer the authentic European training experience that develops complete players.

Performance

Red clay slows the ball and produces a higher bounce, allowing players to develop tactical intelligence and movement mastery. Every rally builds endurance. Every point teaches patience.

Recovery

Training is only half the equation. Our cold plunge pools, guided stretching sessions, and athlete club rooms ensure your body recovers as hard as it trains. Performance compounds through proper recovery.

Frequently Asked Questions

Memberships are annual commitments with monthly payment options. Court booking privileges depend on membership tier. All members must follow club etiquette and court maintenance protocols.
Individual members can book up to 4 hours per week in advance. Family memberships allow 8 hours per week. Founding members receive priority booking and extended limits.
Clay courts require maintenance after rain. Courts are closed during and immediately after precipitation. Members receive notifications about court availability.
Non-marking tennis shoes are required on all courts. Clay-specific shoes are recommended but not mandatory. Street shoes are prohibited on court surfaces.
Family plans cover up to 4 family members. All members must be registered. Junior programme access is included. Guest privileges are shared across the family membership.
